# Design System - SafeBeat Care Landing Page

## Product Context
- **What this is:** A B2B healthcare landing page for SafeBeat Care, focused on antiarrhythmic monitoring workflows for cardiology organizations.
- **Who it's for:** Electrophysiologists, cardiologists, practice administrators, and network operations leaders.
- **Space/industry:** Cardiac monitoring, digital health, and regulated medtech services.
- **Project type:** Marketing site (single-conversion landing page).

## Aesthetic Direction
- **Direction:** Refined clinical-modern.
- **Decoration level:** Intentional.
- **Mood:** Calm, credible, and operationally serious. The interface should feel precise and contemporary without looking consumer-wellness or generic SaaS.
- **Reference sites:** Internal strategy source in `SAFEBEAT_CARE_LANDING_PAGE_RESEARCH.md`.

## Typography
- **Display/Hero:** `Sora` (600/700) - geometric and modern, with enough personality for distinct brand tone.
- **Body:** `Plus Jakarta Sans` (400/500) - highly readable for dense clinical/operations copy.
- **UI/Labels:** `Plus Jakarta Sans` (500/600).
- **Data/Tables:** `IBM Plex Mono` - strong numeric clarity for metrics and regulatory references.
- **Code:** `IBM Plex Mono`.
- **Loading:** Google Fonts.
- **Scale:** 14, 16, 18, 24, 32, 44, 56px.

## Color
- **Approach:** Restrained.
- **Primary:** `#0A7A78` (deep teal) - trust, clinical precision, primary CTAs.
- **Secondary:** `#0E2337` (navy slate) - high-contrast headers and anchors.
- **Accent:** `#D29A2E` (amber) - sparse highlight usage for key metrics/callouts.
- **Neutrals:** `#F6F8FA`, `#E6ECF1`, `#B2C0CC`, `#6B7A88`, `#1E2A36`.
- **Semantic:** success `#1F8A4C`, warning `#9B6A06`, error `#B13A3A`, info `#2B6CB0`.
- **Dark mode:** Keep surface contrast high and reduce accent saturation by 10-15%.

## Spacing
- **Base unit:** 8px.
- **Density:** Comfortable.
- **Scale:** 4, 8, 12, 16, 24, 32, 48, 64, 96px.

## Layout
- **Approach:** Hybrid (grid-disciplined with selective editorial asymmetry).
- **Grid:** Mobile 1-col, tablet 6-col, desktop 12-col.
- **Max content width:** 1160px.
- **Border radius:** 8px cards, 12px large surfaces, 999px pills/chips.

## Motion
- **Approach:** Intentional.
- **Easing:** enter `ease-out`, exit `ease-in`, move `ease-in-out`.
- **Duration:** micro 90ms, short 180ms, medium 280ms, long 420ms.

## Copy Tone
- **Voice:** Clinical, specific, non-hyped, and buyer-practical.
- **Rules:**
  - No universal outcomes (`always`, `guaranteed`, `risk-free`).
  - No implied FDA endorsement.
  - No reimbursement guarantees.
  - Qualify all performance language (`can`, `designed to`, `for eligible patients`).
  - Keep one CTA phrase across the full page.
