## 2) Problem Framing

Antiarrhythmic pathways can require intensive monitoring and operational coordination.  
Inpatient-first processes can constrain capacity, delay access, and increase staff burden.

SafeBeat Care is designed to support selected outpatient workflows under clinician oversight with protocolized monitoring, escalation, and documentation.

## 3) How It Works (4 Steps)

1. Identify eligible patients with your care team.
2. Enroll into a defined monitoring protocol.
3. Review ECG/QT data with escalation rules and human clinical oversight.
4. Final treatment decisions remain with the treating clinician, with documentation support for operations.
