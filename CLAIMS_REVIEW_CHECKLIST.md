## 2) Regulatory and Legal Controls

- [ ] Every regulatory statement is exact and counsel-approved.
- [ ] No line implies FDA endorsement.
- [ ] Cleared vs investigational language is consistent across all sections.
- [ ] Intended use and limitations language is present where required.
- [ ] Off-label implications were reviewed and removed.

## 3) Clinical and Safety Controls

- [ ] Clinical claims map to a citable study or internal evidence packet.
- [ ] Claims use qualified language (`can`, `supports`, `designed to`) unless absolute proof is documented.
- [ ] Eligibility/suitability language is present (`for eligible patients`).
- [ ] Escalation and clinician-oversight statements are present.
- [ ] Emergency-use disclaimer is present.

## 4) Economic and Operations Controls

- [ ] Financial claims avoid guarantees and universal outcomes.
- [ ] Reimbursement language includes variability disclaimer.
- [ ] Time-to-value/implementation claims have an approved assumption set.
- [ ] Any benchmark percentage has source date and owner.

## 5) Privacy, Security, and Intake Controls

- [ ] Intake form excludes patient-identifying clinical narrative fields.
- [ ] Privacy/HIPAA statements were reviewed by compliance owner.
- [ ] Data handling statements match actual system behavior.
